2017 Birmingham Festivals

Name: Stab In The Dark

Strength: 5.00%

Type: Strong Ale




Stab In The Dark Imperial Porter was first brewed for the Great Australasian Beer SpecTAPular 2015 (GABS). As black as midnight, and exhibiting aromas of the morning; coffee, dark chocolate, vanilla and leather. Expect flavours of bitter dark chocolate, mocha, espresso coffee and chocolate liqueur in a rich, smooth as silk body, before a long and spirituous finish. It will reward cellaring up to 5+ years
