"Burton Bridge Brewery is a traditional style brewery that produces a wide range of beers. We deliver within a 40 mile radius of Burton upon Trent on a weekly basis and travel further afield bi-monthly. We have well established contacts with a large number of similar breweries and can obtain a beers from around the country. Ideal if you are running a beer festival. Let us take all the hassel. Call us to discuss your requirements We have stillaging and cooling equipment, will organise a selection of beers for you to approve and set it all up For something a little different ask if we have our vintage Morris dray available. It has been on display at festivals and was used as a hearse for a funeral."
