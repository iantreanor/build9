Charnwood Brewery was founded here in Loughborough, Leicestershire in 2014. I’m Andrew and along with my wife Andrea and our son George we’re making quality, craft, cask conditioned beer for local pubs, clubs and hotels and bottled beer for sale in local shops and restaurants. You can also come and buy our beer direct from the brewery in bottle, 5L mini keg and 10/20L Bag In Box to enjoy at home or for parties and events.
